About Stripe Press

Stripe Press publishes ideas for progress. Books about science, technology, economics, and the forces that shape civilisation. Their catalogue includes new ideas from established and emerging thinkers, alongside reimagined editions of enduring works with renewed relevance today. Every title is beautifully designed, intellectually ambitious, and made for curious, high-agency readers.
